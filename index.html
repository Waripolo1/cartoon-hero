Naruto Uzumaki

About Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki, born in Konohagakure, was a child with tough beginings, as he never met his parents, and had no friends. His hard work, and perseverance, gave him what he always craved: being the hokage. He saves his village in multiple occations, and although not the best leader, he makes a quite good job. People start respecting him, and he becomes the strogest character in the whole Naruto-verse.
